My projects

Dans les pas de Mïa

UNITY - Androïd - 2024 (3 months)

A phygital game playable on tablet with a children's book, in which a parent-child duo help Mïa invite her friends for her birthday.
This game was developped alongside my courses at CNAM ENJMIN and I had the opportunity to work with 2 Game Designers, 2 Game Artist, 3 Sound Designers, and another Programmer.

  • Dialogue system and tool
  • Chapter system
  • All the UI
  • Teaching Git, Unity and the integration pipeline
  • 3 Minigames

Disco Gecko

UNITY - PC - 2024 (3 months)

A 2v2 party game where you have to find your friend in a huge jungle night club.
I worked on this project at the same time I was working on DLPDMïa. I collaborated with 1 Producer, 2 Game Designers, 1 Sound Designer, 2 Composers, 1 UX Designer, 2 Game Artist, and another Programmer.

  • Procedural generation and tool
  • Replay tool
  • Online Features (Team creation, Character customization)

Squeeky

UNREAL ENGINE 5 - PC - 2022/2023 (6 months)

A fast paced 3D plateformer in which you play as Squeeky, a flying Squirel. Race through short levels as fast as possible.
This project gave me the chance to explore various aspects of Unreal Engine and apply as many common practices in video game development as possible.

  • Wind currents and grappling mecanics
  • Particles
  • Achievements
  • 3D Modeling and Animation
  • Menus
  • SCRUM
